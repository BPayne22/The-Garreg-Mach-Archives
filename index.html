The Garreg Mach Archive

Welcome to the Garreg Mach Archive, a fan-made resource dedicated to Fire Emblem: Three Houses. This project collects information, tools, and references to help players explore the world of Fódlan and its characters.

Project Goals

  • Provide a clean, accessible archive of character profiles and stats.
  • Offer interactive tools like the Stat Growth Calculator to plan builds.
  • Maintain an in-game Calendar for events, birthdays, and route-specific details.
  • Create a community-driven resource that is non-commercial and respectful of Nintendo/Intelligent Systems’ work.
